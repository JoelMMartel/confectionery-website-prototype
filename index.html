Welcome!

Beau’s Bonbons has a long standing history with Guelph ON, with its flagship location, which was established in 1942. The Beaus Family has provided the downtown core with organic, handmade sweets and treats. We proudly serve our goods at our locations in Southern Ontario, with our newest location in Dundas, Ontario.
